Why is Tom so fired up about this?

For anyone who knows anything about Tom's Halo career they can all agree on one thing. He puts the team first. He shows this by the extrodinary amount of assists he produces game in and game out. However, the 343 Industries algorithm used to decide worthiness as a Spartan puts little to no emphasis on assists. If it did, then Tom would consistently be ranked higher than Sam and Connor, however that is not the case. Another season, another Platinum 3 rank. Yet my collegues, with significantly less assists than myself and not enough production in any other statistical category to make up for it, are diamonds. There are two possibilites. Either 343 has made El Elegido225 public enemy number 1 and will do whatever it takes to bring him down, or the algorithm used to rank aspiring spartans is seriously flawed. Since I no reason to suspect that El Elegido225 has done anything worth punishment so cruel, it must the the algorithm that is messed up. So, my plan is to revamp the algoritm and make it more accurate by placing more emphasis on things that, you know, actually help the team win, like assists... (Btw Connor, an algorithm is just a series of steps that solve a problem, or in this specific case, a series of calculations to come up with your season ranking)

343 Industries CSR (Competitive Skill Rank) Algorithm

Taken directly from the Halo waypoint website, here is a quote explaining the purpose of the CSR. "The Competitive Skill Rating (CSR) system in Halo 5 is designed to ensure high quality matches between players of equal skill, on a per-playlist basis. When you first hop into any of the Arena playlists, you will play a series of placement matches before being earning your CSR. After you complete 10 games, you will be placed into one of 7 CSR ranks based on your performance."

However, we all know this is bogus because how many times have we got absolutely wrecked by people with "similar" ranking as ourselves? The answer is a lot.

Now, here is the part that really puzzles me. After you get your CSR, the only way to improve it is through wins. You could have 30 kills and 10 assists but if you lose the game, your CSR will not improve. You would think that if they put that much emphasis on winning and playing as a team AFTER you have received your CSR, that they would emphasize team based play and winning while playing in the ranking games. But, obviously they don't.

Tom's CSR Algorithm

As you may have noticed by this point in the website, Tom is very passionate about team play. He really values assists and distraction medals. So his algorithm takes into account many team centered statistics such as:

  • assists
  • damage dealt
  • distraction medals
  • power weapons given up to more skilled players (e.g. rockets)
  • accurate call-outs
  • positive comments to keep team spirits high

Is this the best possible new algorithm to rank spartans? I don't know, you be the judge of that. However, I am sure we can all agree that Tom's algorithm is light-years ahead of 343 Industries algorithm.
